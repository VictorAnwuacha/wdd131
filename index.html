Nigeria, located in West Africa, has a rich history dating back thousands of years. The Nok culture, Hausa-Fulani states, and Yoruba kingdoms like Oyo and Ife shaped its early civilizations. Islam spread through trade in the north, while European contact began in the 15th century, leading to British colonization by 1900. In 1914, the northern and southern regions were amalgamated to form modern Nigeria.
